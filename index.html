Navigator

The game is not rocket science, despite the theme. Navigate between randomly placed tokens like the game "Snake", with the twist of avoiding increasingly unforgiving asteroids- and whatever else may be out there... this is very much a work in progress, and any suggestions or feedback would be welcome :)
